## Understanding Battles

### Battle Types

#### Random Theme
- Any token type allowed
- No restrictions on deployment

#### Mini App Theme
- Must deploy a mini app with token
- App functionality will be verified

#### Meme Theme
- Must be meme-based tokens
- Community voting on meme quality

#### Banker Theme
- Must be banker/utility tokens
- Launched on X (Twitter)

### Battle Checkpoints

Battles have multiple prediction lines:
- **1 Hour**: Quick predictions on early momentum
- **12 Hours**: Short-term performance
- **Day 1**: First full day results
- **Day 1.5**: Mid-battle checkpoint
- **Day 2**: Two-day performance
- **Final**: Week-end results

Each checkpoint is an independent betting opportunity!

## Placing Bets

### Step-by-Step

1. **Choose a Battle**
   - View active battles on the home screen
   - Spotlight battles are featured with higher visibility

2. **Select Checkpoint**
   - Pick which timeframe you want to bet on
   - Earlier checkpoints = faster results but less data

3. **Pick Winner**
   - Choose which token you think will perform better
   - Review current metrics and trends

4. **Set Amount**
   - Minimum bet: $1 worth of $COC
   - No maximum limit

5. **Choose Leverage (Optional)**
   - 1x = No leverage (standard bet)
   - 2x-50x = Multiplied potential returns
   - ‚ö†Ô∏è Higher leverage = higher risk

6. **Confirm Bet**
   - Review all details
   - Approve transaction
   - Wait for confirmation

### Leverage Explained

**Example with 10x leverage:**
- You bet: 100 $COC
- Token wins with 2x odds
- Standard payout: 200 $COC
- With 10x leverage: 2,000 $COC

**But remember:**
- Leverage amplifies both wins AND losses
- Use responsibly
- Start small if you're new

## During the Battle

### What You Can Do

#### Monitor Progress
- Check live token metrics
- View current pool sizes
- Track your bet status

#### Early Cash Out
- Exit your position before battle ends
- Receive 90% of your bet back (10% penalty)
- Cannot re-enter same bet
- Useful if you change your mind

#### Place More Bets
- Multiple checkpoints available
- Bet on different battles
- Cannot change existing bets

### What You Cannot Do
- Change your prediction
- Cancel without penalty
- Increase bet after placement

## After the Battle

### Settlement
- Battles conclude at end time
- Oracle verifies results
- Winners can claim payouts
- Results are final (no appeals)

### Claiming Winnings
1. Navigate to "My Bets"
2. Find completed battles
3. Click "Claim Rewards"
4. Confirm transaction
5. Tokens sent to your wallet

### Payout Calculation

**Example:**
- Total pool: 10,000 $COC
- Your bet: 1,000 $COC
- Winner pool total: 6,000 $COC
- Your share: (1,000 / 6,000) √ó 80% of total pool

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I bet on both tokens?**
A: Yes, but on different checkpoints only.

**Q: What if battle is cancelled?**
A: All bets refunded automatically.

**Q: How are winners determined?**
A: Composite score: Market cap (40%), Volume (30%), Holders (20%), Price (10%)

**Q: Can I change my bet?**
A: No, but you can cash out early with 10% penalty.

**Q: When do I receive winnings?**
A: Claim anytime after battle completion.

**Q: Is leverage risky?**
A: Yes! Higher leverage = higher risk. Use carefully.
